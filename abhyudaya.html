Abhyudaya

At Abhyudaya, first started in 2018, we celebrate the fusion of technology, culture, and creativity, providing students of Punjabi University, Patiala, with a platform to showcase their talent and innovation. The fest is designed to encourage collaboration, inspire curiosity, and foster a spirit of healthy competition, while creating a memorable and inclusive experience for the campus community. Through thoughtfully curated events, performances, and competitions, Abhyudaya empowers students to explore their potential, express their creativity, and build meaningful connections across disciplines.

The Challenge

The development of the Little Angels Public School website presented several key challenges, starting with the need to structure a diverse range of content—academic details, faculty profiles, admission guidelines, and public disclosures—in a way that was intuitive and accessible for both parents and school staff. Achieving a design balance was equally important, as the site had to feel warm and welcoming to families while maintaining a professional tone for stakeholders. Additionally, ensuring responsiveness across all devices was critical, especially for mobile users in rural areas who often faced limited bandwidth and older hardware.

Our Approach

To build the Little Angels Public School website, I leveraged Bootstrap to speed up development and ensure the layout was fully responsive across all screen sizes. I emphasized semantic clarity throughout the site by using well-defined headings, concise content blocks, and intuitive navigation that made it easy for users to find what they needed.
